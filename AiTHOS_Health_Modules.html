Know Your Health Identity

“Your Health Passport starts here. Know what matters before you need it.”

Understand the core information that makes up your health identity and complete the Core Identity section of your Health Passport.

You’ll be able to:
  • Name the five basics in a personal health identity profile
  • Understand why allergies, meds, emergency contacts, and providers matter
  • Complete or update your Core Identity section

Show Up Ready for Care

“Don’t walk into care cold. Show up ready.”

Prepare for visits, explain symptoms clearly, ask better questions, and capture next steps in your Healthograph.

You’ll be able to:
  • Prepare for any appointment with a simple three-question model
  • Describe symptoms so providers understand the issue
  • Create a Visit Prep Card in your Passport

Injury, Recovery & Return-to-Play

“Injury care is part of your performance story. Document it. Learn from it.”

Understand what to document around injuries, recovery milestones, restrictions, and return-to-play — without medical advice.

You’ll be able to:
  • Tell the difference between documenting an injury and self-diagnosing
  • Capture the details that make an injury timeline useful
  • Know the return-to-play questions to ask a clinician or trainer

Own the Health Handoff

“Your parents helped build your health story. Now it starts becoming yours.”

Move from parent-managed health info to self-managed ownership — while keeping support and privacy.

You’ll be able to:
  • Identify the health details parents often hold that you should learn
  • Understand what changes as you become a legal adult
  • Send a Parent Completion Link to strengthen your Passport
